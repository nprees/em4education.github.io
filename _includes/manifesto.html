Manifesto

Exams and Coursework

Fight against exam and coursework deadlines being on the same day or the day after each other.

Extenuating Circumstances

Make life easier when going through extenuating circustances by offering more avenues for student support.

Union Events

Stop any full days of lectures for events such as Varsity and Summer Ball.

Postgraduate Opportunities

Create close connections to charities who fund postgraduate study to give students wishing to further their education more options.

Choosing Seminars

Get rid of set seminar timetables, aiming for shorter gaps between seminars and lectures with more being held on the same day.

Notify Changes

Introduce email notifications for changes in timetables.
